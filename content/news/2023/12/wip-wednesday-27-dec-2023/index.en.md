The FreeCAD Project Association recently approved three new members:

- Stephen Hawes, CEO and founder of [Opulo](https://www.opulo.io/), creators of the LumenPnP desktop pick-and-place machine designed with FreeCAD;

- Paulo Kiefe, founder of [Stemfie](https://www.stemfie.org/), a free & open-source 3D-printable and open-sourced educational construction set;

- Shai Seger, lead developer of [Fasteners](https://github.com/shaise/FreeCAD_FastenersWB) and [Sheet Metal](https://github.com/shaise/FreeCAD_SheetMetal) workbenches.

Extending the general assembly to include founders of FreeCAD-powered businesses and educational projects, as well as developers of popular workbenches will hopefully bring new perspectives to the project.
