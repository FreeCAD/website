The FreeCAD team is happy to announce the release of [FreeCAD 0.21](https://github.com/FreeCAD/FreeCAD/releases/tag/0.21.0)! This release contains thousands of bug fixes and hundreds of other improvements. New Sketcher tools, improved TechDraw features, and significant advances in the finite element method (FEM) workbench are just a few of the many areas developers worked on over the last year. More details can be found on the wiki [v0.21 Release Page](https://wiki.freecad.org/Release_notes_0.21)

Get the latest version at [our downloads page](https://www.freecad.org/downloads.php)!
