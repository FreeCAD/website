_REMINDER_: The first-Sunday-of-the-month FreeCAD Developer's Meeting is this Sunday. Check out [the calendar in your timezone here](https://www.freecad.org/events.php). It's open to anyone interested in FreeCAD development.

This week in GitHub:

- **@davesrocketshop** merged another large chunk of Materials Workbench code, continuing to build up the user interface and Materials information library

- **@wwmayer** has been working on refactoring the section-cutting code, and did some cleanup based on issues identified in [this PVS Studio blog post](https://pvs-studio.com/en/blog/posts/cpp/1072/)

- The team at **Ondsel** continues to work on getting the [Ondsel Solver](https://github.com/Ondsel-Development/OndselSolver) compiling across all platforms so that it can be used in the new integrated Assembly Workbench

- I (**@chennes**) am working on the Toponaming merge, getting the serialization of ComplexGeoData added to main.

**PR stats:** In the week from Wednesday, 25 Oct to Wednesday, 1 Nov there were 23 pull requests merged, with a total codebase change of +5,842 / ‑3,825 lines. We had an average merged PR size of +383/ ‑138. We closed 9 Issues, and 22 new Issues were opened.
